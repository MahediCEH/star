Update on Our Sourcing Process

At Brands by Stareon, we work exclusively as a sourcing and buying agent for our registered buyers. Rather than holding an inventory of stock garments, we follow a unique approach that allows us to source and secure high-quality branded and non-branded garments tailored to each buyer’s requirements.

Here’s how it works:
  • Registered Buyers Only: Buyers must register with us by sharing their requirements and appointing us as their local buying agent.
  • Sourcing on Demand: We source garments based on specific buyer requirements from our network of trusted factories.
  • Quality Checks and Purchase: Once a buyer confirms interest, we conduct quality checks before finalizing the purchase.
  • Flexible Shipping Options: Buyers may opt for us to handle the shipping or arrange it themselves.
​
Our aim is to streamline the garment procurement process and provide a fully customized buying experience. Reach out to learn more about becoming a registered buyer with us.
