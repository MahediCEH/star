About us
Stareon Champion Ltd. is a sister concern of Stareon Group. Stareon Champion Ltd. is the largest chemicals & reagents importer & supplier in Bangladesh for textile industry, dyeing industry & laboratory industry. We distribute or supply board categories of chemicals & reagents in Bangladesh. Such as Dyestuffs, Pretreatment Process, Dyeing Process, Printing Process, Finishing Process, Inkjet Dyes, Organic Pigments, Naphthols, Fast Bases, Color Salts, Inorganic Pigments, Textile Auxiliaries, Optical Brightener, Resist Salt, Caramel Color, Dyes for Soap, Dyes for Detergent, Synthetic Food Colors, Thickeners, Adhesives, Construction Chemicals, Enzymes etc.
