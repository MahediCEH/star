StareonTex Apparel Sourcing

StareonTex is a Professional Apparel/Garment Sourcing Solutions Agency from Bangladesh. Your trusted partner to meet cost, quality and timeliness expectations.
StareonTex Apparel Sourcing is one of the leading professional apparel buying sourcing agency in Bangladesh with a reputation of quality, reliability and unparalleled customer service.

StareonTex has established in 2010 and it has gained tremendous growth for its performance, capacity and commitment towards customers.

Headquartered in the global second largest apparel producing country in Dhaka, Bangladesh; we are still a young & fast growing company, we have the best practices in the industry by the bests in the country by providing outstanding garment buying sourcing solution to our International Garments Buyer, Fashion Brand Retailers, Wholesalers, Distributors, Internet Sellers.

We take orders from buyers and place them to our most trusted as well as potential factories. We do production planning from very initial stage, do follow up, communicate with buyers regularly, inform daily status, do inline and final inspection, control shipping schedule and finally check and dispatch shipping documents to the buyers. We have a unique methodology of paper work that is immaculately followed and managed by our team. Our team consists of highly professionals on ready made garments especially designers, managements, merchandisers, quality controls, schedules and commercial personnel.

We believe our customers are the reasons we exist for without them our service is but null. We want to assure our honorable clients worldwide to keep highest quality with a competitive price. And we strongly believe that to keep commitment is the most important thing to success in long term business as well as a great duty for all human beings. We take the pride in putting a smile on out consumers face across the globe.

WORKING PROCEDURE IN PARTICULAR

For fresh production, we prefer to pursue the following steps :-
  • Buyer provides us the Technical and all details of the objective product.
  • We do the consumption and costing in our office by our own professional, independent of any factory.
  • We offer the buyer the costing that should be feasible and then, if it is necessary, we negotiate to agree to a price.
  • We negotiate with the factories that would be interested in undertaking the order at the price that have been agreed upon with the buyer.
  • We submit to the buyer company profiles of the factories that agree to take the order.
  • Buyer audits and selects the factories.
  • We submit the Pro-forma Invoice.
  • Buyer issues us TRANSFERABLE LC or TT.
  • We transfer the LC to the factory and then...
  • Production process commences.

​QUALITY MANAGEMENT

Orders are closely monitored from sampling to shipment. Status on sampling, production and shipment are transmitted to buyers regularly. We maintain a pro-active approach towards identification of problem areas and corrective measures are taken immediately to ensure timely deliveries. Our quality monitoring system pertaining to production has four stages as explained below:

Pre Production Check
Risks are reduced by checking material components, accessories / user manuals etc. at this stage itself.

Initial Production Check 
First finished products are checked against buyer’s specification and prototype sample. Deviations are identified and brought out for correction.
​
During Production Check
Inspection during production is carried out to check and verify that the initial discrepancies have been rectified and to ensure the average quality standard of production runs.

Final Random Inspection
Final random inspection is carried out when the total consignment is packed and ready for shipment. FRI is performed according to the International Inspection Standards.
The detailed physical inspection of the samples selected at random is based on specifications of the buyers and it cover the criteria such as design / style, shrinkage, accessories, appearance, markings, color, labeling, material, assortments, workmanship, measurements, packing etc.
